FAQ

1. How far in advance should we book you?

It's recommended to book as soon as possible, typically 6-12 months before your wedding date to ensure availability.

2. What is included in your wedding packages?

Our packages include full-day photography, a pre-wedding consultation, edited digital images, and an online gallery. We also offer add-ons like albums and prints.

3. Do you travel for weddings?

Yes, we love destination weddings! Travel fees may apply depending on the location.

4. How long will it take to receive our photos?

You will typically receive your edited photos within 6-8 weeks after your wedding day.

5. Do you offer engagement sessions?

Yes, engagement sessions are included in some of our packages, or can be added as a separate service.
